Following The DAV Transportation Network Token Sale - Everything you need to know

We’re grateful for the success we were able to achieve during our token sale. During our token sale, we successfully raised 40,723 ETH! Thank you to our amazing community for all your ongoing support. Please note, this "Everything you need to know" is meant to be a helpful quick summary only. The actual Terms and Conditions of the token sale are here.

Expected Use Of Funds

45% - Research and Development

15% - Marketing and Business Development

20% - Expansion & Operations

10% - General & Administrative

10% - Legal & Compliance

Token Allocation

  • 40% of the total number of DAV tokens will be allocated to contributors during the token sale (both presale and public sale).
  • 25% of the total number of DAV tokens will be allocated to DAV Foundation to be used for community grants, partnerships and bounties.
  • 20% of the total number of DAV tokens will be allocated to DAV Foundation to be used for future development of the “internet of transportation”, and as a reserve for the company.
  • 15% of the total number of DAV tokens will be allocated to the team and members of the advisory board.

Token Locking

  • All team tokens are locked and gradually released over a 15 month period (linear quarterly cliffs).
  • All reserve tokens are locked and gradually released over a 60 month period (linear yearly cliffs).
  • At least 95% of presale bonuses are locked for periods of 6 to 12 months.

Legal

Thanks to the legal team’s efforts, the DAV Tokens will be classified as utility tokens by the Swiss regulator! With a landscape filled with regulatory uncertainty, we believe DAV is one of the first projects with this improved level of regulatory certainty, which we feel is a monumental accomplishment.

Receiving / Transferring DAV Tokens

Tokens purchased at the main token sale were sent immediately via smart contract to the contributors whitelisted wallet address. Tokens are not transferable for up to 14 days from the end of the token sale.

Final Note

We are deeply grateful to each and every one of you for your support and for sharing the DAV vision with us. As members of the DAV community, you have a leading role in DAV’s development and future. We at the DAV Foundation know that decentralized open-source projects thrive and grow through community contribution and it is only with a strong community can the DAV vision become a reality. We are encouraged by what the future promises and very much look forward to the journey ahead!
